Parabol's Gate

Parabol's Gate is the first and iconic gate of Hanoi University of Science and Technology. Built in 1986, the Parabolic Gate marked the school's development in the early stages of building and expanding facilities. With a unique design based on a parabolic shape - symbolizing precision and creativity in science and technology - the gate represents the school's academic spirit and aspiration to reach far. Located at the gateway of Giai Phong Street, Parabol's Gate is the pride of generations of Hanoi University of Science and Technology’s students.

Tien Lake

Tien Lake is a project associated with the school's development and landscape expansion since its early years of establishment. The lake was built with the purpose of both improving the environment and creating a relaxing space for the campus community. This is also the place to organize many extracurricular activities, take commemorative photos, and become a familiar symbol for generations of Hanoi University of Science and Technology’s students.

TQB libary

Ta Quang Buu Library, built in 2006, is one of the largest libraries in Southeast Asia, serving as a modern learning resource center of Hanoi University of Science and Technology. With an area of ​​more than 37,000 m², the library stands out by its unique architecture, integrating self-study areas, group meeting rooms, and in-depth research spaces. The library's characteristic feature is a rich document system with more than 500,000 books, modern digital documents and services such as borrowing books via applications and electronic searches. This is not only a place to provide knowledge but also a symbol of culture, creativity and innovation of the school, attracting students and researchers from all over the country.

HANOI UNIVERSITY OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY

1. General introduction

Hanoi University of Science and Technology (English name Hanoi University of Science and Technology - abbreviated HUST) was established in 1956. This is the first technical university in our country with the mission of training industrial engineers for industry. the construction of socialism in the North and the struggle to liberate the South.

Hanoi University of Science and Technology is a multi-disciplinary and multi-field science and technology training and research center; Closely combine training with scientific research to create high-quality human resources and foster scientific and technological talents; oriented to develop into a research university on par with prestigious universities in the region and the world.

2. The Areas

Hanoi University of Science and Technology has 4 gates, with 2 streets and 5 surrounding roads. The total area for teaching, scientific research and conference halls is up to 26.2 hectares, Maximum width among fields in content. Many students have to look up Google Maps right at their main school.
Among the 4 gates, the Parabol's gate on Giai Phong Street is a symbol and also the pride of Polytechnic students when studying under the most prestigious and modern school.
Ta Quang Buu Library is a project commemorating the 50th anniversary of the establishment of Hanoi University of Science and Technology, with a total construction investment of more than 200 billion VND.
